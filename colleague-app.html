What MoodLoop Staff demonstrates. The same closed loop as the customer edition, redesigned for colleagues: anonymous by default with an enforced anonymity floor, quick shift pulses at staff-area Loop devices, issue raising, colleague recognition and an ideas channel. The loop closes to the team through the You said / we did wall, and to an individual only when they opt in to a confidential reply. Data is in-memory and resets on refresh.
